My Interests

I am passionate about computer vision and artificial intelligence.

One of the computer vision projects I worked on is QBot. QBot is a mobile, 3D-printed robot powered by an Android phone. Its design is based off of Qualcomm's Snapdragon Micro Rover. My team and I modified it to participate in Carnegie Mellon's 2015 Mobot Races, which we ended up winning first place. Our Android apk, which includes the line-tracking algorithm we deployed in the robot, can be found here.

Neo is a project that uses artificial intelligence to operate. He is an personal assistant for Android that communicates through a homemade 24x8 LED signboard. To learn more about him and what he can do, feel free to see him in action here.
